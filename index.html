Record straight from your wrist.

Offline-first

Works without a signal.

Logging a workout on a flight, in the mountains, in a basement gym? Kinetopia just works. Most competitors silently degrade when offline — you can't see your history, your map won't load, your splits don't render. Kinetopia is the same in airplane mode as it is on LTE.

📵
Zero network calls
Search the binary for HTTP. You won't find it. The app does not contain any networking code that talks to a server we own — because no such server exists.
🗺️
Maps cache as you go
Apple Maps caches tiles you've already viewed. Your familiar routes will render offline; new ground needs a signal once to populate.
🎯
All charts, all PRs, offline
Splits, elevation, heart-rate, weekly distance heatmap, personal records, achievements, goal progress — all computed locally from your data.
🛡

Truly private.

No accounts. No analytics SDKs. No tracking pixels. No Facebook, Mixpanel, Amplitude, Firebase, Segment, Sentry, PostHog. Your workouts never leave this device — unless you explicitly turn on iCloud sync, in which case they sync through your own private iCloud, never a third-party server.
